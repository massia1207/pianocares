About Piano Cares

Providing piano tuning and basic repairs to music lovers in the DC Metro Area. Marcus Assia is a member of the Piano Technicians Guild and trained in aural tuning with the International Piano Technicians School. Marcus's focus is on improving your instrument's performance through precise tuning, targeted repairs and detailed regulation of the mechanical action. Each service appointment aims to improve your piano's sound quality and overall playing experience.
